# Risk Assessment Methodology

## ISO 27001 Clause 6.1.2 - Risk Assessment & Risk Register

### Risk Assessment Approach

EugTech Solutions Ltd uses a documented and repeatable risk assessment process in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 6.1.2.

Information security risks are identified, analysed, evaluated, and treated using a 5×5 risk scoring matrix.

### Risk Scoring Method

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

#### Likelihood Scale

| Score | Description    |
| ----- | -------------- |
| 1     | Rare           |
| 2     | Unlikely       |
| 3     | Possible       |
| 4     | Likely         |
| 5     | Almost Certain |

#### Impact Scale

| Score | Description |
| ----- | ----------- |
| 1     | Negligible  |
| 2     | Minor       |
| 3     | Moderate    |
| 4     | Major       |
| 5     | Critical    |

### Risk Matrix

| Likelihood \ Impact | 1 | 2  | 3  | 4  | 5  |
| ------------------- | - | -- | -- | -- | -- |
| 5                   | 5 | 10 | 15 | 20 | 25 |
| 4                   | 4 | 8  | 12 | 16 | 20 |
| 3                   | 3 | 6  | 9  | 12 | 15 |
| 2                   | 2 | 4  | 6  | 8  | 10 |
| 1                   | 1 | 2  | 3  | 4  | 5  |

### Risk Classification

| Score Range | Classification |
| ----------- | -------------- |
| 1–8         | Low Risk       |
| 9–15        | Medium Risk    |
| 16–25       | High Risk      |

### Treatment Decisions

Each identified risk must have one of the following treatment decisions:

* Accept
* Treat
* Transfer
* Avoid

### Residual Risk

Following implementation of controls, residual risk shall be assessed and documented to determine whether the remaining risk level is acceptable.

### Review Requirements

Risk assessments shall be reviewed periodically and whenever significant business, technology, supplier, regulatory, or operational changes occur.
