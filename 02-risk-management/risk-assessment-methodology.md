# Risk Assessment Methodology

## Purpose

This methodology defines how EugTech Solutions Ltd identifies, analyses, evaluates, and treats information security risks within the Information Security Management System (ISMS).

## Risk Assessment Process

### Step 1: Identify Assets

Examples include:

- Information
- Applications
- Cloud Services
- Devices
- Personnel
- Suppliers

### Step 2: Identify Threats

Examples include:

- Malware
- Phishing
- Data Breach
- Insider Threat
- Human Error
- Service Outage

### Step 3: Identify Vulnerabilities

Examples include:

- Weak Passwords
- Unpatched Systems
- Lack of Training
- Poor Access Control
- Inadequate Monitoring

### Step 4: Assess Likelihood

| Score | Description |
|---------|---------|
| 1 | Rare |
| 2 | Unlikely |
| 3 | Possible |
| 4 | Likely |
| 5 | Almost Certain |

### Step 5: Assess Impact

| Score | Description |
|---------|---------|
| 1 | Insignificant |
| 2 | Minor |
| 3 | Moderate |
| 4 | Major |
| 5 | Severe |

## Risk Calculation

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

| Score Range | Risk Level |
|------------|------------|
| 1-4 | Low |
| 5-9 | Medium |
| 10-16 | High |
| 17-25 | Critical |

## Risk Treatment Options

- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Accept
- Avoid

## Review

Risk assessments are reviewed annually or following significant business, technology, or regulatory changes.
