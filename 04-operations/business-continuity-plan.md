# Business Continuity Plan

**Organisation:** EugTech Solutions Ltd
**ISO 27001 Reference:** Annex A 5.29

## Purpose

The purpose of this Business Continuity Plan (BCP) is to ensure that critical business services can continue or be restored following a disruption that affects normal operations.

## Scope

This plan applies to:

* Cloud consultancy services
* Managed IT services
* Internal corporate systems
* Microsoft 365 services
* Azure-hosted infrastructure
* Remote working operations

## Business Continuity Objectives

* Protect personnel and information assets
* Maintain critical client services
* Restore affected systems within agreed recovery targets
* Minimise operational and financial impact
* Meet contractual and regulatory obligations

## Critical Services

| Service                     | Priority |
| --------------------------- | -------- |
| Client Cloud Infrastructure | Critical |
| Microsoft 365               | Critical |
| Azure Subscription Services | Critical |
| VPN Infrastructure          | High     |
| Source Code Repositories    | High     |
| Internal Corporate Systems  | Medium   |

## Recovery Strategy

### Loss of Cloud Services

* Engage cloud provider incident response process
* Restore services using backup and recovery procedures
* Notify affected clients

### Loss of User Devices

* Issue replacement devices
* Restore user data from approved backups
* Re-enrol device into MDM

### Loss of Office Facilities

* Activate remote working arrangements
* Continue operations using cloud-hosted systems

## Backup Requirements

* Daily backups
* Weekly full backups
* Quarterly restore testing

## Recovery Objectives

| Objective                      | Target              |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------- |
| Recovery Time Objective (RTO)  | Defined per service |
| Recovery Point Objective (RPO) | Defined per service |

## Testing and Review

The Business Continuity Plan shall be tested at least annually and reviewed following significant business, technology, or operational changes.
