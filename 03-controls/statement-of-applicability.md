# Statement of Applicability (SoA)

**Organisation:** EugTech Solutions Ltd
**ISO 27001 Clause:** 6.1.3

## Purpose

The Statement of Applicability is the document an ISO 27001 auditor examines most closely. It lists Annex A controls and states whether each is implemented, partially implemented, not implemented, or not applicable, together with supporting justification and evidence.

This Statement of Applicability covers a representative selection of Annex A controls across all four themes and demonstrates the methodology used to determine applicability and implementation status.
