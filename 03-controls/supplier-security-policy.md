# Supplier Security Policy

**Organisation:** EugTech Solutions Ltd
**ISO 27001 Reference:** Annex A 5.19

## Purpose

This policy defines the information security requirements that apply to all suppliers, vendors, contractors, and third parties that provide services to EugTech Solutions Ltd or have access to company information, systems, or client data.

## Policy Requirements

### Supplier Assessment

All suppliers shall undergo an information security assessment before engagement.

The assessment should consider:

* Information security controls
* Regulatory compliance
* Data protection obligations
* Incident management capabilities
* Access control practices

### Contractual Security Requirements

Supplier agreements shall include:

* Confidentiality obligations
* Data protection requirements
* Security responsibilities
* Incident reporting requirements
* Right-to-audit clauses where appropriate

### Access Control

Third-party access shall:

* Be approved by management
* Follow the principle of least privilege
* Be reviewed regularly
* Be removed when no longer required

### Monitoring and Review

Suppliers shall be reviewed periodically to ensure continued compliance with security requirements.

Security concerns identified during reviews shall be documented and tracked to completion.

### Incident Reporting

Suppliers must report any actual or suspected security incident that could affect EugTech Solutions Ltd or its clients without undue delay.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Information Security Manager

* Maintain supplier security requirements
* Review supplier risk assessments
* Monitor supplier compliance

### Contract Owners

* Ensure suppliers comply with contractual security obligations
* Escalate security concerns

### Suppliers

* Comply with agreed security requirements
* Notify EugTech Solutions Ltd of relevant security incidents
