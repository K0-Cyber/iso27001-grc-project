# Information Security Policy
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** January 2026  
**Review Date:** January 2027
## Purpose
EugTech Solutions Ltd is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information belonging to the organisation and its clients. This policy establishes the framework for managing information security risks across all operations.
## Commitment

EugTech commits to:

- Implementing and maintaining an Information Security Management System (ISMS) aligned to ISO/IEC 27001:2022.
- Identifying and managing information security risks through a structured risk assessment process.
- Complying with applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual information security requirements.
- Providing information security training and awareness to all staff.
- Continually improving the effectiveness of the ISMS through regular review and internal audit.
## Scope
This policy applies to all EugTech employees, contractors, and third parties who access company or client information systems. Full ISMS scope is defined in the ISMS Scope Statement document.
## Policy Statements
- All information assets must be classified, inventoried, and assigned an owner.
- Access to information must be granted on a least-privilege basis and reviewed quarterly.
- All security incidents must be reported immediately to the Information Security Manager.
- Remote and home-working is permitted subject to the Remote Working Policy.
- Supplier and third-party access to company data must be governed by a signed agreement.
## Responsibilities
### Board of Directors
Approve this policy and provide resources for ISMS implementation.

### Information Security Manager
Maintain the ISMS, conduct risk assessments, and manage incidents.

### All Staff
Comply with this policy and all supporting security procedures.
