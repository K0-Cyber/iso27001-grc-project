# ISMS Scope Statement

## Organisation
EugTech Solutions Ltd

## Scope Statement

The ISMS of EugTech Solutions Ltd encompasses all information assets, processes, systems, and people involved in the design, delivery, and support of cloud consultancy and managed IT services to external clients.

### Included

- All cloud infrastructure operated on behalf of clients (Azure, AWS tenants)
- Internal corporate IT systems (Microsoft 365, Azure AD, laptops, mobile devices)
- Source code repositories and development environments
- All 50 employees and contractors operating within UK jurisdiction
- Physical premises and home-working environments

### Exclusions

- Personal devices not used for company business
- Client-owned infrastructure where EugTech has no administrative control
